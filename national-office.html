BGBU National Office

Leadership and Administration for the Brigade in Uganda

National Council

The National Council is the supreme decision-making body of the Brigade, responsible for developing policies, overseeing activities, and ensuring the organization fulfills its mission.

Composition of the National Council

  • National President (Chairperson)
  • Field Officers of all Battalions
  • 2 elected representatives from each Field Office
  • Other officers and key leaders

Executive Committee

The Executive Committee manages the day-to-day operations of the Brigade and implements decisions of the National Council.
