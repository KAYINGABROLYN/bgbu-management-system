About the Global Fellowship

The Boys' and Girls' Brigade of Uganda is proud to be part of the Global Fellowship of Christian Youth Organizations, an international network of uniformed youth organizations sharing similar Christian values and objectives.

Through this fellowship, BGBU members have opportunities to connect with peers worldwide, participate in international events, and share best practices in Christian youth development.

Apply for International Programs

BGBU members can apply to participate in various international programs through the Global Fellowship. The application process includes:

  1. Review program requirements and deadlines
  2. Complete the online application form
  3. Submit required documents (recommendation letters, etc.)
  4. Participate in selection interviews
  5. Complete pre-departure training if selected

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum 2 years as active BGBU member
  • Good standing in local unit
  • Age requirements vary by program
  • Demonstrated leadership potential
  • Commitment to Christian values
