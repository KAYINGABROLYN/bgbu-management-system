About the East African Fellowship

The East African Regional Fellowship is a collaborative platform for Brigade organizations in Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, and South Sudan. We come together to share best practices, organize joint events, and strengthen our collective impact on youth development in the region.
