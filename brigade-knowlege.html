The Four Square Program: Developing the whole person

The Four Square Program

The Four Square Program is the core curriculum of the Boys' and Girls' Brigade of Uganda, designed to develop members in four key areas: Spiritual, Educational, Physical, and Social. This holistic approach ensures balanced growth for all members.

Spiritual

Developing Christian character and faith

Educational

Acquiring knowledge and skills

Physical

Developing health and fitness

Social

Building relationships and community

Spiritual Square

The Spiritual Square focuses on developing a strong Christian foundation through Bible knowledge, prayer, worship, and Christian service.

Key Activities

  • Weekly Bible study and devotionals
  • Scripture memorization
  • Prayer meetings and retreats
  • Christian character development
  • Worship and praise sessions
  • Evangelism and outreach

Badge Program

The BGBU Badge Program recognizes members' achievements in each of the Four Squares. Members earn badges by completing specific requirements in each area.

How the Badge Program Works

  1. Members select badges they wish to earn from the Four Square categories
  2. They work with their unit leaders to complete the requirements
  3. Upon completion, badges are awarded at special ceremonies
  4. Badges are displayed on the member's uniform
  5. Advanced badges are available for those who master basic skills
