Registration FAQs

New member registrations are typically processed within 7-10 working days. You will receive your official BGBU Member ID via SMS or email once processed. Subscription payments for existing members are processed within 3-5 working days.

Yes, you can register in person at any BGBU company unit. Local leaders have paper registration forms and can assist with the process. Your information will then be submitted to the national registry.

For junior members (under 18), we require parental consent which can be provided digitally during online registration or via a signed form for in-person registration. Officers and volunteers must provide two references and may need to complete additional screening.

Fee waiver requests are reviewed by your local battalion leadership. You will be contacted via phone or email within 2 weeks of submission. If approved, you'll receive confirmation and can participate fully without payment. If denied, you'll be given options for payment plans or other assistance.

If you've lost your Member ID, contact your company or battalion leader who can look up your information in our system. You can also email registry@bgbu.org with your full name, phone number, and battalion information to request your ID.
