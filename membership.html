Membership Categories

The Boys' and Girls' Brigade of Uganda welcomes members from all backgrounds who share our values and commitment to youth development.

Junior Members

Ages 6-12 years

  • Participate in age-appropriate activities
  • Develop foundational Christian values
  • Engage in fun, educational programs

Senior Members

Ages 13-18 years

  • Leadership development opportunities
  • Advanced training programs
  • Mentorship and career guidance

Officers

Adult leaders (18+ years)

  • Mentor and guide young members
  • Organize and lead programs
  • Voting rights in Brigade decisions

Honorary Members

Special recognition

  • Individuals who support BGBU's mission
  • Significant contributors to the Brigade
  • Approved by the National Council

Volunteers

Support roles

  • Assist with programs and events
  • No formal leadership responsibilities
  • Flexible participation

Membership Benefits

Spiritual Growth

Develop a strong Christian foundation through Bible study, prayer, and worship.

Leadership Training

Gain practical leadership skills through our structured programs.

Community Service

Opportunities to serve and make a difference in your community.

Friendship & Networking

Build lifelong friendships with peers across Uganda and beyond.

Skill Development

Learn practical skills in various areas including first aid, public speaking, and more.

Career Guidance

Access to mentorship and career development resources.

How to Join

1

2

3

Complete Registration

Fill out the membership form and submit required documents.

4

Pay Membership Fee

Submit the annual subscription fee (waivers available for those in need).

5

Start Participating

Begin attending meetings and activities as a full member.

Membership Requirements

For Junior & Senior Members

  • Age between 6-18 years
  • Parental/guardian consent for minors
  • Commitment to participate in Brigade activities
  • Willingness to abide by Brigade values and code of conduct

For Officers

  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • Good standing in the community
  • Commitment to Christian values
  • Willingness to undergo training
  • Clear background check

Membership FAQs

Membership fees are affordable and vary by category. Junior members pay UGX 10,000 annually, senior members UGX 15,000, and officers UGX 20,000. Fee waivers are available for those with financial constraints.

Yes, while BGBU is a Christian organization, we welcome members of all faiths who respect our Christian values and are willing to participate in our programs.

Activities include weekly meetings with Bible study and skill-building, community service projects, camping and outdoor activities, leadership training, and participation in regional and national events.

Most company units meet weekly, typically on Saturdays. Additional activities and events are scheduled throughout the year.

BGBU has official uniforms for different membership categories. Uniform costs vary but are kept affordable. Some units have uniform banks to assist members who cannot afford their own.
