Subscription Fees

Annual membership fees help support the operations of the Brigade at all levels, from local company units to national programs. Fees are kept affordable and waivers are available for those with financial constraints.

Junior Members

UGX 10,000/year

Ages 6-12 years

  • Weekly meetings
  • Basic training
  • Activity participation
  • Badge program
Most Popular

Senior Members

UGX 15,000/year

Ages 13-18 years

  • All junior benefits
  • Leadership training
  • Advanced programs
  • Service opportunities

Officers

UGX 20,000/year

Adult leaders

  • Officer training

Fee Waiver Program

BGBU believes financial constraints should not prevent anyone from participating. Fee waivers are available for members who demonstrate need. Contact your local unit leader or membership@bgbu.org for more information.

Subscription FAQs

Annual subscriptions are due at the beginning of each calendar year (January). New members joining during the year pay a prorated amount.

Subscription fees support Brigade activities at all levels: 40% remains with your local company, 30% supports battalion/district programs, 20% goes to regional initiatives, and 10% supports national administration and programs.

Yes, installment payments can be arranged through your local company treasurer. Please discuss payment plans with your unit leader.

For online payments, receipts are automatically emailed. For mobile money or bank payments, send proof of payment to finance@bgbu.org to receive a receipt. Cash payments should be accompanied by an immediate receipt from the receiving officer.

BGBU never wants finances to prevent participation. Fee waivers and scholarships are available. Contact your unit leader or email membership@bgbu.org to confidentially discuss options.
