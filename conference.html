Conference Registration

Registration Process

  1. Complete the online registration form below
  2. Pay the registration fee through provided methods
  3. Receive confirmation email with event details
  4. Print or save your registration ticket
  5. Bring ticket and BGBU ID to conference check-in

Registration Fees

Member Category Early Bird (Before 1 month) Regular On-Site
Junior Members UGX 25,000 UGX 30,000 UGX 35,000
Senior Members UGX 35,000 UGX 40,000 UGX 45,000
Officers UGX 50,000 UGX 60,000 UGX 70,000

Fees include conference materials, meals, and accommodation. Transportation not included.

Scholarships

Limited scholarships are available for members with financial need. Contact your battalion commander for application information at least 2 months before the conference date.
