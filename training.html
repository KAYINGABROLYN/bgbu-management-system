BGBU Training Programs

Developing skills and character for life and leadership

Our Training Philosophy

BGBU's training programs are designed to develop the whole person - spiritually, mentally, and physically. Our curriculum combines Christian values with practical skills to prepare members for leadership in all areas of life.

Training is offered at different levels to meet the needs of junior members (ages 6-12), senior members (13-18), and officers (adult leaders).
